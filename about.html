Background

Who I Am

I'm Izaz Sarbudeen, an Electrical Planning & Design Engineer with hands-on experience delivering major EPC projects across Saudi Arabia — for clients including Saudi Aramco and SEC (Saudi Electricity Company).

My engineering journey began in Tamil Nadu, India, where I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical & Electronics Engineering. After graduating, I moved into industrial quality control and ERP coordination before transitioning into full-scale site engineering and project planning in Saudi Arabia.

Today I specialise in Overhead Transmission Lines (OHTL), 110kV Underground Cable systems, high-voltage commissioning testing (VLF, IR, PD, RMU), and Oracle Primavera P6 project scheduling — applying Earned Value Management and CPM scheduling to keep complex multi-discipline projects on time and on budget.

Outside engineering, I build web tools, automation bots, and dashboards — bridging the gap between heavy industry and modern digital workflows. I believe the best engineers understand both the field and the data behind it.

⚡ Precision Engineering

Every transmission line, cable route, and schedule I deliver is engineered to exact standards — SAES, SEC, and international codes, with zero compromise on quality.

📊 Data-Driven Planning

I use Primavera P6, EVM metrics, and real-time reporting to give project stakeholders full visibility — not guesswork, but measured performance data.

🦺 Safety First, Always

Certified in BLS, confined space supervision, gas testing, and Saudi Aramco PTW — I lead by example on every site.

🤖 Digital Workflows

I build the tools that make engineering work smarter — dashboards, bots, and automation that reduce manual effort and increase accuracy.

🌍 Global Mindset

Multilingual professional (English, Tamil, Hindi, Arabic) with experience across Indian manufacturing and Saudi Arabia's oil & gas sector.
